Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This spaghetti carbonara is a super rich "bacon and egg" pasta dish that's great to serve for company.
This recipe also makes an unusual brunch offering.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound paghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 8 diced slices bacon
  • 1 choppe onion
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 4 beaten large eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of slightly salted water, stirring occasionally about 12 minutes.
  2. Drain and toss spaghetti with 1 tablespoon oil.
  3. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir around 10 minutes.
  4. Drain bacon on paper towels and reserve 2 tablespoons bacon fat in the skillet.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon oil to bacon fat in the skillet, add chopped onion and cook over medium heat.
  6. Add minced garlic and cook about 1 minute, add wine and cook for one more minute.
  7. Return cooked bacon to the skillet, add cooked spaghetti. Add more olive if it seems dry or sticks together.
  8. Add beaten eggs and cook, then add ½ cup Parmesan cheese and toss.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Serve with chopped parsley on top and extra Parmesan.
