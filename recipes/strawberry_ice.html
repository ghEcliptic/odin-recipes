Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

Strawberry ice cream is super easy to make with ripe juicy strawberries and this recipe that doesn't use an egg-custard base but still tastes rich and creamy.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • Optional food coloring

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Transfer the mixture to the freezer bowl.
  3. Freeze until firm.
